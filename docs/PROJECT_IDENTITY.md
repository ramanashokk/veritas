# Project Identity

Version: 1.0

---

# What is Veritas?

Veritas is an Evidence Intelligence Platform.

It helps people understand what the current body of evidence supports, how strong that evidence is, and why.

It is designed to make scientific evidence transparent, explainable and reproducible.

---

# What Veritas is NOT

Veritas is NOT:

• an AI chatbot

• a search engine

• a medical advisor

• a fact-checking website

• a replacement for scientific judgment

AI may assist Veritas, but AI is never the authority.

Evidence is.

---

# Who is Veritas for?

Primary users:

• Researchers

• Healthcare professionals

• Journalists

• Policy makers

• Students

• Curious individuals who want evidence instead of opinions

---

# Problems Veritas Solves

People often receive:

- conflicting articles

- conflicting news

- conflicting research papers

- AI answers without citations

- information without context

Veritas organizes evidence into understandable conclusions.

---

# What Makes Veritas Different

Most systems answer questions.

Veritas evaluates evidence.

Most systems generate opinions.

Veritas explains methodology.

Most systems hide uncertainty.

Veritas exposes uncertainty.

Most systems summarize.

Veritas justifies.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Become the world's most trusted evidence intelligence platform.

Trust should come from:

- transparency

- methodology

- reproducibility

—not from the language model.

---

# Design Philosophy

Architecture should outlive technology.

Models should be replaceable.

Providers should be replaceable.

AI should be replaceable.

Methodology should remain.

---

# Non-Goals

Veritas will never optimize for:

- sensational answers

- engagement

- popularity

- advertising

- persuasion

- emotional manipulation

Accuracy and transparency always come first.

---

# Product Philosophy

Every feature must answer one question:

Does this help users understand evidence better?

If the answer is no, the feature should not exist.

---

# Future Vision

One day Veritas should become the platform people trust when they need to understand scientific evidence—not because it speaks confidently, but because it shows its work.

---

# Final Principle

Trust is earned.

Trust comes from evidence.

Evidence comes before everything else.
