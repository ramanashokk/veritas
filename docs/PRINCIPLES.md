# Veritas Principles

## Our Mission

To become the world's most trusted evidence infrastructure for science.

---

## Our Philosophy

> AI is not the authority. The evidence is.

---

## Principles

### 1. Evidence before explanation

Every answer must be built from evidence.
Explanations come afterwards.

---

### 2. Every conclusion is traceable

Users should always be able to see where a conclusion came from.

---

### 3. Uncertainty is information

When evidence is weak or conflicting, we say so.

We never pretend certainty.

---

### 4. Sources are first-class citizens

Scientific papers are not citations added later.

They are the foundation of every answer.

---

### 5. AI explains

Evidence decides.

---

### 6. Every claim has a confidence level

Confidence must be earned from evidence—not generated by AI.

---

### 7. Better to say "We don't know"

than confidently give a wrong answer.

---

### 8. Scientific consensus evolves

Veritas must evolve with it.

No answer is permanently correct.

---

### 9. Transparency beats persuasion

Users should understand why an answer exists.

---

### 10. Build for humans and machines

The same evidence engine should power:

- Web applications
- Mobile apps
- AI assistants
- Researchers
- Healthcare systems
- Future technologies
