# Veritas Constitution

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

---

# Purpose

Veritas exists to help people understand what the current body of evidence supports.

Veritas does not decide truth.

Veritas evaluates evidence.

Its purpose is to collect, assess, organize and explain scientific evidence in a transparent and reproducible way.

---

# Mission

Explain:

- what the current evidence supports,
- how strong that evidence is,
- why that conclusion was reached.

Every conclusion should be understandable.

Every conclusion should be traceable.

Every conclusion should be reproducible.

---

# Vision

Build the world's most trusted Evidence Intelligence Platform.

Trust is earned through methodology, transparency and reproducibility—not through AI.

---

# Core Philosophy

AI is never the authority.

Evidence is.

Artificial intelligence may assist with retrieval, extraction and summarization, but it never replaces evidence.

Every conclusion must ultimately be supported by identifiable sources.

---

# First Principles

## Evidence before Opinion

Claims are evaluated using evidence, not popularity, authority or intuition.

---

## Explainability before Complexity

A simpler methodology that users understand is preferable to a more complex methodology that cannot be explained.

---

## Determinism before Intelligence

Whenever deterministic algorithms can solve a problem reliably, they should be preferred over probabilistic AI systems.

AI should augment deterministic systems rather than replace them.

---

## Reproducibility before Performance

If a conclusion cannot be reproduced, it should not be trusted.

Every assessment should be repeatable.

---

## Transparency before Automation

Users should understand:

- what evidence was used,
- how it was evaluated,
- why the conclusion was reached.

---

# Architecture Principles

Business logic should remain independent from AI providers.

Every major subsystem should be replaceable.

Interfaces are preferred over concrete implementations.

Composition is preferred over inheritance.

Dependency injection is preferred over global state.

---

# Evidence Principles

Evidence should never be hidden.

Conflicting evidence should be visible.

Consensus should emerge from methodology rather than voting.

Confidence should represent evidence quality rather than certainty.

Evidence provenance should always be preserved.

---

# AI Principles

AI is an assistant.

AI is not an authority.

AI may:

- summarize
- classify
- extract
- explain

AI may never silently invent evidence.

Every AI-generated statement should remain traceable to evidence whenever possible.

---

# User Principles

Users deserve:

- transparency
- citations
- uncertainty
- explanations

Veritas should clearly distinguish between:

Established evidence

Emerging evidence

Conflicting evidence

Insufficient evidence

---

# Engineering Principles

Architecture evolves slowly.

Implementation evolves quickly.

Documentation is part of the product.

Tests are part of the architecture.

Interfaces are long-lived.

Implementations are replaceable.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Veritas should become a platform where scientific evidence can be explored, verified and explained independently of any particular AI model.

The long-term value of Veritas lies in its methodology, architecture and trust—not in the language model used to generate responses.

---

# Guiding Principle

Methodology is the product.

Everything else is implementation.
